Our Balanced Diet Plans

Keto Diet

The ketogenic diet is high-fat, moderate-protein, and very low-carb, shifting the body into ketosisâ€”a state where stored fat is broken down into ketones for energy instead of carbs. This continues until carbs are reintroduced.

Mediterranean Diet

The Mediterranean diet, popular in Greece, Italy, and Spain, focuses on whole foods like vegetables, fruits, whole grains, legumes, and lean proteins, with minimal processed foods. It includes olive oil, herbs, and small amounts of red wine, limiting red meat and sweets.

Plant-Based Diet

The diet consists primarily of food made of plants: fruits and vegetables in addition to nuts, seeds, whole grains, and legumes.
