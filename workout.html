Fitness Workout

Pushups

The push-up, or press-up, is a calisthenics exercise that strengthens the pectoral muscles, triceps, and anterior deltoids by raising and lowering the body from a prone position.

Jogging

Jogging is a slow-paced run aimed at boosting fitness with less body stress than fast running and more than walking, making it ideal for sustained aerobic endurance over long distances.

Barbell lunge

The barbell lunge is a leg exercise that targets muscles in the lower body, including the hamstrings, quadriceps, and glutes.

Crunches

The crunch, or curl-up, is an abdominal exercise targeting the rectus abdominis, helping to build and define "six-pack" abs. Though convenient and low-cost, crunches are less effective than planks and may carry a risk of back injury.
